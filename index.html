When permits apply

Permits Required

Permits are required at all times except University-recognized holidays and weekends from Friday 5 PM through Sunday 11 PM.

Important rule

Overnight parking

Kiosk overnight parking between 2 AM and 5 AM must be in Lot 19. Online visitor permits do not include overnight parking.

AMP pay-by-space

Short-term zones

AMP spaces exist in Lots 1, 2, 3, 4R, 7, 8, 11, 12, 12A, 14, and 23, plus Hyer, Koshkonong Drive, and Lauderdale Drive.

How time limits work

Session caps

Most AMP areas allow up to 2 hours. Lots 11 and 23 allow up to 4 hours. After the limit, that zone locks for 24 hours.

Important permit rule

AMP Stalls

A parking permit does not waive AMP fees. If a stall is marked for AMP pay-by-space, payment is still required.

Resident permit warning

Yellow Lots

Visitors need to stay out of all-yellow resident lots. No permit allows parking in a full yellow lot. Yellow resident lots include 2R, 8, 17, 20, and 22. Some resident-area lots do allow visitor use in limited ways: Lots 18 and 19 accept visitor parking permits, while Lots 8 and 4R are AMP meter parking only.

Plate visibility rule

License Plates

Vehicle registration plates that are not attached to the front or rear of the vehicle, fictitious, decorative, or vanity plates, or plates that are damaged, sun faded, worn, or illegible because of debris are not acceptable and must be corrected. Vehicles issued only one plate, regardless of state, must be parked with that plate facing the drive lanes of the parking lot.
